Journey Insights

The Journey Insights page is your personal dashboard for discovering fascinating patterns, statistics, and achievements based on your tracked location data. It transforms your raw GPS data into a compelling story of your travels and daily life.

Accessing Journey Insightsâ€‹

You can find the Journey Insights page in the main navigation menu of the application.

Featuresâ€‹

The page is divided into several sections, each offering a unique perspective on your journeys.

Geographic Adventuresâ€‹

This section visualizes the geographic scope of your travels.

  • Countries Explored: See a count of all the countries you've visited. The list displays each country's flag and name.
  • Cities Visited: Discover how many cities you've been to. Each city in the list is shown with its name and the number of times you've visited it.

Your Travel Storyâ€‹

Dive into the statistics of your travel distances.

  • Total Distance Traveled: Your overall distance covered across all modes of transport. It's accompanied by a fun, motivational phrase that puts the distance into perspective.
  • Distance by Car: The total distance you've traveled by car, shown as a value and as a percentage of your total travel.
  • Distance Walking: The total distance you've covered on foot, also shown as a value and as a percentage of your total travel.

Time Patternsâ€‹

Uncover the temporal patterns of your activity.

  • Most Active Month Ever: Highlights the calendar month where you were most active.
  • Current Month Performance: Compares your activity in the current month to the previous one.
  • Busiest Day of Week: Shows which day of the week you are typically most active.
  • Most Active Time of Day: Reveals the time of day you are usually on the move.

Your Journey Milestonesâ€‹

This section is all about your achievements. You can track your progress towards earning various badges.

  • Progress: A summary of how many available badges you have earned.
  • Badges: A collection of all possible achievements. Each badge has:
    • An icon, title, and description.
    • A progress bar showing how close you are to earning it (if not yet earned).
    • An "Earned!" label and the date of achievement (if earned).
info

Achievement Calculation: Non-completed achievements are re-calculated every 30 minutes. All achievements are calculated from scratch when new data is imported via the Import page or during Timeline Regeneration.
